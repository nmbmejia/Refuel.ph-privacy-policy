Privacy Policy

refuel.ph — your simple and practical fuel companion

Last updated: April 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to refuel.ph! We built this app to help you track every fuel-up, understand your real km/L, and finally see where your gas budget is going. To make that work, we handle a small amount of your data — and we take that seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why we need it, and how we protect it.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect what's necessary to make refuel.ph useful to you. This includes:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email address and authentication credentials to keep your data synced across devices.
  • Fuel Log Data: Every fuel-up you log — including date, odometer reading, fuel volume, price per liter, and fuel station — is stored so you can review your history and track your spending over time.
  • Vehicle Information: Details you enter about your vehicle (e.g., make, model) used to organize your fuel records.
  • App Usage Data: Anonymized data on how the app is used, to help us improve features and fix bugs.

3. Why We Need These Permissions

Here's a straightforward breakdown of what the app accesses and why:

  • Internet Access: Required to sync your fuel logs and account data so your history is never lost if you switch phones.
  • User Authentication: Lets you securely log in and keep your fuel data private to your account.
  • Local Storage: Allows the app to work offline and save your logs even without a connection — syncing happens when you're back online.

refuel.ph does not access your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other device permissions beyond what is listed above.

4. Data Protection

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your fuel logs, spending history, and account details are yours. We store them securely and access them only to provide you with the service.

5. Third-Party Services

refuel.ph does not currently integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, your fuel logs and personal information will be permanently removed from our systems.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of refuel.ph after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

Have questions or concerns about your data? Send us a message on Facebook: facebook.com/refuelph.
