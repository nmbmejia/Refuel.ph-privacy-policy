Privacy Policy

refuel.ph — your simple and practical fuel companion

Last updated: April 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to refuel.ph! We built this app to help you track every fuel-up, understand your real km/L, and finally see where your gas budget is going. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled — and the short version is: it never leaves your device.

2. What Data We Collect

refuel.ph does not collect any data. The app works entirely offline. All information you enter — including fuel logs, odometer readings, prices, and vehicle details — is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers, no accounts, and no cloud storage.

3. How Your Data is Stored and Exported

Your fuel records are saved directly on your device. You can export your data at any time as a CSV file, which you can open in any spreadsheet app. You are in full control — if you uninstall the app or clear its data, your records will be permanently deleted from your device.

4. Permissions

refuel.ph only requires access to local device storage to save and read your fuel logs. It does not access your location, contacts, camera, microphone, internet connection, or any other device permissions.

5. Third-Party Services

refuel.ph does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, authentication, or data-sharing services. No data is ever sent to external servers.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of refuel.ph after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
